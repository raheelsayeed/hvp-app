Human Values Project

The Human Values Project (HVP) is an international research initiative exploring how artificial intelligence systems can align with human values in medical decision-making. Led by Dr. Isaac Kohane at Harvard Medical School, this effort is currently in its foundational phase.

As AI becomes more integrated into clinical workflows, ensuring its alignment with patient values is crucial. HVP's initial focus is on designing systematic methods to study real-world decision-making across diverse clinical environments.

Key Publications

  • Medical Artificial Intelligence and Human Values (NEJM, 2024)
  • Compared with What? Measuring AI against the Health Care We Have (NEJM, 2024)
