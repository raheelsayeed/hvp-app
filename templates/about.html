About the Human Values Project

The Human Values Project (HVP) is an emerging international research initiative examining the alignment between artificial intelligence systems and human values in medical decision-making. Led by Dr. Isaac Kohane at Harvard Medical School, this work is currently in its foundational phase.

As AI increasingly influences clinical decisions, understanding and implementing proper value alignment becomes crucial for patient care and safety. Our initial focus is on developing systematic approaches to studying clinical decision-making across diverse healthcare contexts.

Key Publications

  • Medical Artificial Intelligence and Human Values (NEJM, 2024)
  • Compared with What? Measuring AI against the Health Care We Have (NEJM, 2024)

Current Status

The Human Values Project is currently in its initial phase. We are developing our research frameworks and will be announcing several key initiatives in the coming months. Updates will be posted here as they become available.
